How it works

Up and running in minutes

No setup complexity. No long implementation. Start with built-in templates, customise as you grow.

1
Set up your document sets
Built-in templates for NL ZZP, NL Employed (Blue Card), BE Employed, and AT Employed. Every required document is pre-configured. Create fully custom sets for any country or worker type.
2
Add your workers
Assign each worker a document set. Upload their documents, set expiry dates, and get instant compliance status. The system calculates GO, WARNING, and NO-GO automatically.
3
Stay on top automatically
Daily email digests flag everything expiring, missing, or unbilled. Workers submit updates through their own portal. Your compliance team reviews and approves in one place.
Compliance Status

Compliance status. At a glance.

Every worker has a real-time status that tells you exactly whether they are safe to deploy — no guesswork, no manual checking.

✅
GO
All required documents are valid and current. This worker is cleared for deployment on any project. Status updates automatically as documents approach expiry.
⚠️
WARNING
One or more documents are expiring within your configured warning window (default 30 days). Action is needed soon — the worker can still be deployed but needs attention.
🚫
NO-GO
One or more required documents are missing or expired. This worker should not be deployed until the issue is resolved. The exact missing documents are clearly listed.
Features

Everything your compliance team needs

One platform that replaces the spreadsheets, the WhatsApp groups, and the manual email reminders.

Live Compliance Status
Real-time GO / WARNING / NO-GO for every worker, automatically recalculated as documents expire. No manual refresh required.
Document Tracking
Track every required document per worker with expiry dates, issue dates, and uploaded file copies stored securely. One-click download any time.
Worker Portal
Workers get their own secure link to submit and update documents in English, Dutch, or Romanian. No account creation needed.
Project Gantt
Visual chart showing who is assigned to which projects across what time periods. Spot scheduling conflicts and coverage gaps instantly.
Accommodation & Vehicle Billing
Track housing and vehicle assignments with a weekly billing tracker. Never miss a billable week — amber cells flag uncharged periods automatically.
Daily Email Digest
Automated morning email listing everything expiring, missing, or unbilled. Sent to your compliance team every day at 07:00. No email if nothing to report.
Role-Based Access
Admin, Planner, Compliance, and Viewer roles. Each user sees and can edit only what their role permits — no accidental changes, no data leaks.
Document Set Templates
Built-in sets for NL ZZP, NL Employed, BE, and AT with all legal documents pre-configured. Fully customisable — add, rename, or remove any document.
Archive & Audit Trail
Soft-delete archive of every change, deletion, and document update. Searchable, filterable, and fully restorable — essential for compliance audits.
