Simple by design
Up and running in three steps.
No lengthy implementation. No consultant required. Just a clear, purposeful setup that gets your team compliant fast.
1
Set up your document sets
Choose from built-in compliance sets for NL ZZP, NL Blue Card, Belgian employed, and Austrian workers — or create custom sets for any worker type. Each set defines exactly which documents are required, with names, categories, and expiry tracking.
2
Add your workers
Add workers with their type, nationality, and assigned document set. Workers immediately get a GO/WARNING/NO-GO score based on their document status. Invite them to the worker portal so they can upload documents directly from their phone.
3
Stay on top automatically
Every morning, a compliance digest lands in your inbox — listing expired documents, upcoming expirations, assignments ending soon, and uncharged billing weeks. The dashboard updates in real time as documents are submitted and approved.
Platform capabilities
Everything compliance teams actually need.
One platform that replaces spreadsheets, WhatsApp chains, and missed deadlines — built specifically for construction labour management.
🚦
GO / WARNING / NO-GO Status
Instant compliance scoring for every worker based on document completeness and expiry dates. Green means ready to work. Red means grounded. Configurable warning windows per document type.
📄
Document Tracking
Track every required document per worker — passport, VCA, A1 form, BSN, KvK, VOG, health insurance and more. Upload files, set expiry dates, and review submissions from the approval queue.
📱
Worker Self-Service Portal
Workers log in with their work email — no app download, no account creation. They see their document status, upload missing files, and check their assignment. Available in English, Dutch, and Romanian.
📅
Project Gantt & Assignments
Gantt-style roster view across all projects and workers. Manage current, upcoming, and historical assignments with start/end dates, rates, and contract file storage. See who is where at any point in time.
🏠
Accommodation & Vehicle Billing
Track accommodation and vehicle assignments per worker. Weekly billing tracker flags uncharged periods automatically. Record invoices, charge references, and operative deductions — all in one place.
📧
Daily Email Digest
Every morning, a summary email covers expired or missing documents, expiry warnings within 60 days, assignments ending within 14 days, and uncharged billing weeks. Nothing slips through unnoticed.
🔐
Role-Based Access Control
Admin, planner, compliance officer, and viewer roles. Each role sees and can edit only what they need. MFA available for all accounts. New users require admin approval before gaining access.
📋
Document Sets & Templates
Four built-in compliance sets covering NL ZZP, NL Blue Card, Belgian, and Austrian workers. Build custom sets for subcontractors or specialist workers. Attach downloadable form templates for each document type.
🗂️
Archive & Audit Trail
Every change is tracked. Deleted items go to a recoverable archive with full payload. Approval and rejection history is retained with timestamps. Pass any labour authority audit with confidence.

Compliance at a glance
Three statuses. Instantly clear.
Every worker gets a live status score based on their document completeness and upcoming expirations. No ambiguity — just clear signals you can act on.
✓ GO
GO — Ready to Work
All required documents are present, current, and not expiring within the warning window. This worker can be assigned to any project with full confidence. Compliance score: 100%.
⚠ WARNING
WARNING — Action Required Soon
All documents are present but one or more are expiring within the configured warning window (default 60 days). Take action now to avoid this worker becoming a NO-GO before the deadline passes.
✕ NO-GO
NO-GO — Cannot Work
One or more required documents are missing or have expired. This worker should not be assigned to any site until their compliance is restored. A daily digest alert is sent until the issue is resolved.
