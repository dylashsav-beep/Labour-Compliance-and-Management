Platform features
Everything you need for labour compliance
One platform to manage worker documents, project assignments, accommodation, vehicles, and billing — all in one place.
✅
GO / NO-GO Status
Instant compliance status for every worker. Red, amber, and green indicators based on document completeness and expiry.
📄
Document Tracking
Track all required documents per worker type — KvK, VOG, VCA, contracts, permits, and more. Expiry alerts built in.
📅
Project Assignments
Gantt-style assignment view across all projects. See who's on what, when assignments end, and manage multiple projects at once.
🏠
Accommodation & Vehicles
Track property and vehicle assignments per worker. Billing tracker for weekly charges — accommodation and fleet vehicles.
📱
Worker Self-Service Portal
Workers submit documents directly from their phone — no app download required. Multi-language support (EN, NL, RO).
📧
Daily Compliance Digest
Automated daily email summary of expiring documents, ending assignments, and uncharged billing weeks. Never miss a deadline.
🔐
Role-Based Access
Admin, planner, compliance, and viewer roles. Each role sees exactly what they need — nothing more.
🌍
Multi-Nationality Support
Document sets for NL ZZP, Blue Card, Belgian, and Austrian workers. Custom sets for any worker type.
☁️
Cloud-Native & Secure
Built on Supabase with row-level security, MFA support, and encrypted file storage. Your data stays yours.
